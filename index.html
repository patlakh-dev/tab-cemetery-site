Tabs come and go. Only the worthy survive.

A Chrome extension that reaps forgotten tabs and lays them to rest in a beautifully haunted graveyard. Resurrect them whenever you want.

How It Works

From setup to cemetery — here's the full ritual.

Set the countdown

Choose how many idle days before the Reaper comes. 3, 7, 40 — your call.

Protect the worthy

Whitelist domains, toggle the death bell, notifications, badge and epitaphs.

Tabs meet their fate

Buried with a tombstone and epitaph. Resurrect with one click or let them rest.

Track the carnage

Souls collected, kill rate, resurrections, oldest grave and haunted domains.

Earn your glory

Unlock First Blood, Serial Killer, Necromancer and keep the killing streak.

Total control

Purge the entire graveyard or mass resurrect every tab back to life.

Features from Beyond

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

The Reaper

Automatic tab closing after your chosen number of days. Silent, efficient, merciless.

The Graveyard

Every dead tab gets a tombstone. Search, filter by domain, sort by age or lifespan.

Resurrection

One click to bring a tab back from the dead. Or resurrect them all at once.

Achievements

Unlock achievements like First Blood, Serial Killer, Necromancer, and more.

Epitaphs

Each dead tab gets a unique epitaph. Domain-specific dark humor for 30+ sites.

Immortals

Whitelist domains that should never be touched. Gmail, Docs — whatever you need alive.

Kill Streaks

Track your daily burial stats and keep the killing streak going.

Death Bell

An optional sound effect and browser notification each time a tab meets its end.

The Graveyard

Tombstones, epitaphs, and last words — all in one place.
